  • Opening Stacked Decks

Opening Stacked Decks

Stacked Decks are consumable items that grant a single random Divination card when opened. Opening Stacked Decks and recording every card you receive is sufficient to collect data on the weight of Divination cards.

  1. Obtain Stacked Decks: Acquire Stacked Decks through gameplay (drops, league mechanics, or trading)
  2. Open Stacked Decks: Use each Stacked Deck to receive one random Divination card
  3. Record Results: Note every Divination card you receive.
  4. Continue Collecting: The more Stacked Decks you open and record, the more accurate your dataset will be

Note: It is recommended to collect at least 10000+ opened Stacked Decks for basic results, and 100000+ for better accuracy.
